MLFF stands for "Multi Lane Free Flow" which is a non-stop toll payment process, where the toll road users do not need to stop their vehicles at the toll gate to make payments. To add a payment method in your Cantas account, you can follow these steps :
1. Select ""Account"" menu
2. Select ""Payment Service""
3. Click the ""Add Payment Method"" button
4. Select the payment method of your choice
5. You can now use the payment method of your choice
