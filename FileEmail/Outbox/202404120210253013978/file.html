The weaknesses of MLFF are as follows:

1. GNSS-based MLFF is highly dependent on the GSM cellular network (3G/4G/5G) along the toll road.

2. There must be an additional OBU in the vehicle or e-OBU in the smartphone.

3. Not all toll road users have mastered IT technology on GNSS-based smartphones.

4. There is always a balance in the e-wallet.

5. There is always internet data on smart devices when on the toll road.

 

However, along with the development of time and technology, some of these weaknesses will be resolved with further socialization so that other people can easily use the MLFF system
