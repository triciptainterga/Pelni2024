We understand your question about how to add vehicle and to share vehicle to other Cantas user. To add a vehicle in your Cantas account, you can follow these steps:

1. Select the "Account" menu

2. Select the "Vehicle" menu

3. Click the "Add Vehicle" button or the Plus sign

4. Select the toll category that matches your vehicle

5. Enter your vehicle information, then click "Next"

6. Upload photos of your front and rear vehicle, then click "Next"

7. Upload photos of your front and back vehicle registration document

8. Click "Finish"

 

You can also send "Share Vehicle" invitation with other users with these steps:

1. From main menu, select "Invitation"

2. Select "Check my shared vehicle"

3. Select vehicle that you want to share with other users in "My Vehicles"

4. Select "Invite Users"

5. Enter the username you want to invite and the expiration date for the invitation.

6. Click "Send Invitation"
