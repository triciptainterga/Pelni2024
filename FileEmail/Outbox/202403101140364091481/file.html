We can inform you that everyone can register a Cantas account. The minimum system requirements for Cantas app are android 10 or above for android phone, or IoS 13 or above for iphone. in order to register an account, you can follow these steps:

1. Click the "Sign up" button
2. Click the "Agree" button for Terms & Conditions
3. Fill in and complete your personal information (Name, Date of Birth, Address, User Name, Password)
4. Enter your phone number that is connected to your Whatsapp 
5. You will receive an OTP (One Time Password) code sent to your Whatsapp number.
6. Enter the OTP code to your Cantas application
7. Your Cantas account is ready to use.

Once you created an account, you can setup payment methods from your account menu. Currently, the only available payment provider is the Digiroad which you can top up from either BJB Bank or QRIS code for other banks.
