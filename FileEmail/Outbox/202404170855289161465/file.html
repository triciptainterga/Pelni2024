Regarding Mr Rama question, We would like to inform you that currently it is not possible to change license plate in Cantas application, if you want to change your plate number you can delete your registered vehicle and add your vehicle again with right license plate, We inform that you can delete a vehicle in the Cantas by following these steps:
1. Go to menu "Account"
2. Select "Vehicle"
3. Select the vehicle you want to delete
4. Select "Delete Vehicle" at the bottom
5. Select "Yes, Delete"

We would like to inform you to add a vehicle in your Cantas account, you can follow these steps:
1. Select the "Account" menu
2. Select the "Vehicle" menu
3. Click the "Add Vehicle" button or the Plus sign
4. Select the toll category that matches your vehicle
5. Enter your vehicle information, then click "Next"
6. Upload photos of your front and rear vehicle, then click "Next"
7. Upload photos of your front and back vehicle registration document
8. Click "Finish"
