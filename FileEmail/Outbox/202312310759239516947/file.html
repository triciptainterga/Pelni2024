Happy holidays and Happy New Year!

Ajay from GMass here, fresh off getting attacked by Krampus in Austria and meeting the Grinch in New York. And also King Kong, who's not an evil Christmas character but apparently is a photo bomber.

According to SaaS law we need to put together a year-in-review. It's not as sexy as Spotify's, but unlike them, we don't suffer from premature self-adulation. (Come on Spotify, releasing a year-in-review on December 1st? I'm 11/12ths impressed. Kidding! You're great, use GMass.)

Anyway...

Here's the best of GMass in 2023!

New features that you love (or you will love, now that you're finding out about them)

We released more than 100 new features and improvements to GMass during the year. As always, all of them were based on what you've told me you want and need. And I'm happy and exhausted to say you really pushed me this year.



Here are some of the highlights:
  • MultiSend brings inbox rotation to GMass — distribute your campaign across multiple email accounts.
  • Triggered emails automatically go out when a recipient opens, clicks, or replies to your campaign — so you know they're in their inbox.
  • The Reply Project isTHE fastest tool ever invented to help you plow through your inbox.
  • GMass now instantly detects replies and bounces. (This one almost broke me.)
  • With DKIM settings you can perfect your email authentication as you send through a third-party SMTP server.
  • Delivery routes help you fix deliverability issues with certain email service providers and domains.
  • Auto follow-up sequences can now stop on "reply OR click" rather than just one or the other.
  • When you use a past GMass campaign as a template, you now have the option to import all settings from that campaign.
  • Easy social sharing to show off the results of your best campaigns.
We even launched a new page on the GMass website where you can see all GMass updates, big and small.

But we did so much more than launch new features

Features are great. Everyone loves features. But plenty of other stuff happened at GMass outside of our new features.



Here are some of the other big developments from inside the GMass multiverse...
  • We launched our own in-house SMTP server. If you're sending opt-in emails, apply to use it!
  • We hired Shine, one of the top deliverability experts in the world, as GMass's new Head of Deliverability.
  • We went live for a handful of great Q&A sessions on Facebook Live in the GMass Facebook group. (More of these to come in 2024!)
  • Our affiliate program continues to grow at a breakneck pace. We also added an easier signup process, a dashboard for monitoring your results, and payouts via Payoneer.
