We can inform you that currently there is no limit for vehicles that can be registered in 1 account. But make sure you have added different of plate number of your vehicle in Cantas if you have more than 1 vehicle.

 

To add a vehicle in your Cantas account, you can follow these steps:

1. Select the "Account" menu

2. Select the "Vehicle" menu

3. Click the "Add Vehicle" button or the Plus sign

4. Select the toll category that matches your vehicle

5. Enter your vehicle information, then click "Next"

6. Upload photos of your front and rear vehicle, then click "Next"

7. Upload photos of your front and back vehicle registration document

8. Click "Finish"
