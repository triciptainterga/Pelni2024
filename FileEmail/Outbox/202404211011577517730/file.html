Regarding your question. We can inform you please be informed that the registered license plate cannot be changed, we recommend deleting the vehicle and registering it again. To delete a vehicle in Cantas by following these steps:
 1. Enter the "Account" menu 
2. Select "Vehicle"
 3. Select the vehicle you want to delete
 4. Select "Delete Vehicle" at the bottom
 5. Select "Yes, Delete"

Next you can add vehicles in the Cantas application, here are the steps that need to be done:
1. Select the "Account" menu
2. Select the "Vehicle" menu
3. Click the "Add Vehicle" button or Plus Sign
4. Select the Toll category that matches your vehicle
5. Enter your vehicle information, then click "Next"
6. Upload front and back photos of your vehicle. then click "Next"
7. Upload a photo of the front and back of your vehicle registration.
8. Click "Finish"
