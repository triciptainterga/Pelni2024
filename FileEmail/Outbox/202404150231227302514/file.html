If you want to delete your cantas account, here are the steps: 

1. From main menu, select "Account"
2. Select "Delete account"
3. Select "Delete my account"
4. Enter the OTP code sent to your Whatsapp

 

Please note that the process of deleting account will take 15 days. You can cancel this process anytime within those 15 days by logging in again. After deleting the account, information related to travel history, fine history, and other information previously recorded in your account will also be deleted.
