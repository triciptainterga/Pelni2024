Thank you for contacting Cantas email service.

We understand the question you have raised in your previous email. We can tell you that the way it works is as follows:

- Toll road users register on the Cantas application.
- Toll road users must press "Start" before traveling.
- The user's position when entering and exiting the toll road will be obtained from GNSS (Global Navigation Satellite System) technology.
- When exiting the toll road, users must press "End" and the application will calculate the fee based on the starting and ending positions, then the balance will be deducted automatically.
- The function of the gantry is to identify violations, license plates, vehicle categories and the number of vehicles passing by.

If you are an expatriate, you will only need to download the Cantas app via PlayStore (minimum Android 10) and AppStore (minimum IOS 13). We apologize that both the app and website are currently unavailable as they are still under development. When available, Cantas will be announced through the official media of PT Roatex Indonesia Toll System (RTIS) and the official media of the Ministry of Public Works and Housing (PUPR).

You can still use payment methods such as E-Money, Flazz, or TapCash when making payments at toll gates for now and later when Cantas has been launched. When Cantas is launched, there will be one special toll gate that can be used.
