Thank you for contacting Cantas email service.

We may informed that to create a Cantas account, you can follow these steps:

1. Click the "Sign up" button

2. Click the "Agree" button for Terms & Conditions

3. Fill in and complete your personal information (Name, Date of Birth, Address, User Name, Password)

4. Enter your phone number that is connected to your Whatsapp

5. You will receive an OTP (One Time Password) code sent to your Whatsapp number.

6. Enter the OTP code to your Cantas application

7. Your Cantas account is ready to use

And to add a vehicle in your Cantas account, you can follow these steps:

1. Select the "Account" menu

2. Select the "Vehicle" menu

3. Click the "Add Vehicle" button or the Plus sign

4. Select the toll category that matches your vehicle

5. Enter your vehicle information, then click "Next"

6. Upload photos of your front and rear vehicle, then click "Next"

7. Upload photos of your front and back vehicle registration document

8. Click "Finish"

And then to add a payment method in your Cantas account, you can follow these steps :

1. Select "Account" menu

2. Select "Payment Service"

3. Click the "Add Payment Method" button

4. Select the payment method of your choice

5. You can now use the payment method of your choice
