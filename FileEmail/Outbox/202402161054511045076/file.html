Cantas is an application designed with (MLFF) or Multi Lane Free Flow system that utilize (GNSS) or Global Navigation Satellite System technology which allows vehicle users to make payments just by using their device without having to stop.

To create a Cantas account, you can follow these steps:

1. Click the ""Sign up"" button

2. Click the ""Agree"" button for Terms & Conditions

3. Fill in and complete your personal information (Name, Date of Birth, Address, User Name, Password)

4. Enter your phone number that is connected to your Whatsapp

5. You will receive an OTP (One Time Password) code sent to your Whatsapp number.

6. Enter the OTP code to your Cantas application

7. Your Cantas account is ready to use

To add a payment method in your Cantas account, you can follow these steps :

1. Select "Account" menu

2. Select "Payment Service"

3. Click the "Add Payment Method" button

4. Select the payment method of your choice

5. You can now use the payment method of your choice

OBU (On-board unit) is a device in a vehicle used to transmit and collect data. An electronic device is installed in the vehicle to record driving data. Commonly used for automated toll collection and recording, known as electronic toll collection.
