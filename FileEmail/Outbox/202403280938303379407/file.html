We can inform you that there are 5 groups of vehicle you can register in your Cantas account. For vehicles like trucks, you can choose between these groups:

1. Group 2 (trucks with 2 axles)
2. Group 3 (trucks with 3 axles, including trailer cars)
3. Group 4 (trucks with 4 axles)
4. Group 5 (trucks with 5 axles or more)
