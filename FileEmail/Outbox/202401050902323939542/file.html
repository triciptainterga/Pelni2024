Yes Mrs Aqila MLFF is new toll payment system. MLFF stands for "Multi Lane Free Flow" which is a non-stop toll payment process, where the toll road users do not need to stop their vehicles at the toll gate to make payments by using CANTAS Apps.

To use MLFF, you need the CANTAS application which can be downloaded after launching in March 2024. Wait for the good news, ma'am

After you can downloaded in March at Goggle Playstore dan iOS App Store, you can follow these steps:
1. Click the "Sign up" button
2. Click the "Agree" button for Terms & Conditions
3. Fill in and complete your personal information (Name, Date of Birth, Address, User Name, Password)
4. Enter your phone number that is connected to your Whatsapp 
5. You will receive an OTP (One Time Password) code sent to your Whatsapp number.
6. Enter the OTP code (One Time Password) to your Cantas application
7. Your Cantas account is ready to use
