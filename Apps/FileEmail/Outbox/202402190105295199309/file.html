The difference between the old toll system and MLFF are:

1. With the old system you need to stop at the toll gate to pay, with MLFF or Cantas app, you no longer need to stop to pay the toll.

2. The old toll system creates queue at the toll gates, with MLFF or Cantas app, there will be no more queueing at the toll gate.

3. By no longer having queues, there will be no more traffic jams near toll gates which will ultimately reduce fuel waste and air pollution.

4. Compared to the old toll system, you can now track your travel history in detail from the Cantas app

To create a Cantas account, you can follow these steps:

1. Click the ""Sign up"" button

2. Click the ""Agree"" button for Terms & Conditions

3. Fill in and complete your personal information (Name, Date of Birth, Address, User Name, Password)

4. Enter your phone number that is connected to your Whatsapp

5. You will receive an OTP (One Time Password) code sent to your Whatsapp number.

6. Enter the OTP code to your Cantas application

7. Your Cantas account is ready to use

You can suggest to pull over first to get an internet connection and also you can still use previous payment methods (like e-money, flazz, brizzy, etc.)
