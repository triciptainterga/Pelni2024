According your question about MLFF (Multi Lane Free Flow), we could inform you for now the system you are referring to is the MLFF (Multi Lane Free Flow) system, which is a toll payment system without having to stop the vehicle. MLFF (Multi Lane Free Flow) technology is present in Indonesia and MLFF (Multi Lane Free Flow) also is a first technology to implement a contactless or cashless toll payment system, in another word is pay tolls without stopping based on an application called the CANTAS application for all classes of vehicle types. The MLFF (Multi Lane Free Flow) system uses Global Navigation Satellite System (GNSS) digital technology that allows toll road user trips to be known through GPS on smart phones. For additional information, an internal trial of the MLFF (Multi Lane Free Flow) system has been tested in Bali at the Ngurah Rai Toll Gate (GT), Bali Mandara Toll Road on December 15 2023, and in the future it will gradually be implemented on all toll roads in Indonesia.
