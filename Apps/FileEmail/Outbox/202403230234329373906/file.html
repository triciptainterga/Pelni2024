We can inform you how to delete the vehicle number plate on the Cantas application as follows:

1. Enter the Cantas Application 

2. Select the 'Account' menu 

3. Select 'Vehicle'

4. Select and Delete Vehicle

5. Press 'Yes'.

And regarding the question of adding vehicles to the CANTAS application, sir / ma'am Mamotampan To add a vehicle in the Cantas application, the following steps need to be taken:
1. Select the "Account" menu
2. Select the "Vehicle" menu
3. Click the "Add Vehicle" button or Plus Sign
4. Select the Toll category that matches your vehicle
5. Enter your vehicle information, then click "Next"
6. Upload front and back photos of your vehicle. then click "Next"
7. Upload a photo of the front and back of your vehicle registration.
8. Click "Finish"
