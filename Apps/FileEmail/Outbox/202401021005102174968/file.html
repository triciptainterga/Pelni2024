Regarding your question about how to share vehicles on Cantas, we can inform you the steps to do it as follows:

1. Login to your Cantas account
2. Select 'account' menu
3. Select a vehicle you want to share
4. Click 'Invite user' button
5. Enter the username of the recipient

You can also decide the duration of your vehicle sharing.

Once you've finished, the recipient can accept your invite from their 'account' menu, and click the 'invitation' button
