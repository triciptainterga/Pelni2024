We can inform you that for now the system you are referring to is the MLFF (Multi Lane Free Flow) system, which is a toll payment system without having to stop the vehicle. MLFF technology is present in Indonesia and is the first technology to implement a contactless cashless toll payment system or pay tolls without stopping based on an application called the CANTAS application for all classes of vehicle types. The MLFF system uses Global Navigation Satellite System (GNSS) digital technology that allows toll road users' trips to be known through GPS on smart phones.

To create a Cantas account, you can follow these steps:

1. Click the ""Sign up"" button

2. Click the ""Agree"" button for Terms & Conditions

3. Fill in and complete your personal information (Name, Date of Birth, Address, User Name, Password)

4. Enter your phone number that is connected to your Whatsapp

5. You will receive an OTP (One Time Password) code sent to your Whatsapp number.

6. Enter the OTP code to your Cantas application

7. Your Cantas account is ready to use

To add a vehicle in your Cantas account, you can follow these steps:

1. Select the "Account" menu

2. Select the "Vehicle" menu

3. Click the "Add Vehicle" button or the Plus sign

4. Select the toll category that matches your vehicle

5. Enter your vehicle information, then click "Next"

6. Upload photos of your front and rear vehicle, then click "Next"

7. Upload photos of your front and back vehicle registration document

8. Click "Finish"

At Cantas, we have several different levels of fine, determined by the time after a violation:

Level 1 Fine: This is issued immediately after a violation occurs.

Level 2 Fine: Issued after the fine is not paid within 2 to 10 days after the violation.

Level 3: 1.5 toll rates for a duration of more than 10x24 hours until an indefinite time. At this level, toll gates will not open and STNK renewal will be blocked.

If you have any questions regarding a fine you received, please provide us with additional details.
