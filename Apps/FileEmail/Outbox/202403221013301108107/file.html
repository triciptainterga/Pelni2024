Well Mr. Cesar, we can inform you that the first phase trial or transition period of MLFF is planned in Bali because the number of toll roads in Bali is not as many as the toll roads in Java and Jakarta. And the mobility on the Bali Toll Road is not as dense as in Jakarta, so we conducted a limited trial for toll users.  During the transition period, the MLFF system will be implemented in several sections and divided into two stages, namely: 
The first stage along the Nusa Dua - Ngurah Rai - Benoa Toll Road (Bali).
Second phase along Prof. Dr. Ir. Sedyatmo Toll Road, Inner City Toll Road, Jagorawi Toll Road, JORR Toll Road. 
So we hope that in the future this MLFF system will be implemented in stages on all toll roads in Indonesia.
