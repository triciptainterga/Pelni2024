Here are the steps to use the Cantas app:
1. Download the Cantas App on the Play Store or App Store
2. You register by entering your personal data and vehicle data
3. Select and connect with the payment method in the application
4. Ensure sufficient balance with a minimum balance of Rp 20,000 in the Cantas application in order to travel
5. Ensure that the vehicle selected in the application matches the one in use
6. Make sure you have selected "Start Trip" before entering the toll road
7. The system will detect when your vehicle crosses the toll road then the balance in the application will be deducted automatically according to the toll tariff that is passed.

And then for the Cantas application must be connected to the internet network when used and will not be able to be used if the internet is off.
