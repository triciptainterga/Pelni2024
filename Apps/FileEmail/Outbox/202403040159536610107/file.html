We understand your question regarding the differences between the current toll system and MLFF, and also when the MLFF service will be released in Indonesia. We can inform you that the differences between the old toll system and MLFF are:

1. With the old system you need to stop at the toll gate to pay, with MLFF or Cantas app, you no longer need to stop to pay the toll.

2. The old toll system creates queue at the toll gates, with MLFF or Cantas app, there will be no more queueing at the toll gate.

3. By no longer having queues, there will be no more traffic jams near toll gates which will ultimately reduce fuel waste and air pollution.

4. Compared to the old toll system, you can now track your travel history in detail from the Cantas app

 

The release of the Cantas application will be announced through the official media of PT. Roatex Indonesia Toll System (RITS) and the official PUPR media. Please wait fot the latest information from us.
