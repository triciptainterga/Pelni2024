You can register your personal ID dan vehicle data in Cantas app.  You can also use Cantas after you have registered your personal ID, vehichle and ensure your sufficient balance. 

Here are the steps to use the Cantas application:
1. Download the Cantas App on the Play Store (minimum Android 10) or App Store (minimum IOS 13)
2. Register by entering your personal data, telephone number connected to the Whatsapp application and vehicle data 
3. Select and connect with the payment method in the application
4. Ensure sufficient balance with a minimum balance of Rp 20,000 in the Cantas application in order to travel
6. Ensure that the vehicle selected in the application matches the one being used
7. Make sure you have selected "Start Trip" before entering the toll road
8. The system will detect when your vehicle crosses the toll road then the balance in the application will be deducted automatically according to the toll tariff that is passed.
