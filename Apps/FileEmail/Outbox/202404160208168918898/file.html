Related to your question, we can inform you to top up the balance through Digiroad using QRIS for bank account users in Indonesia. Here's how: 
1. Select the "Payment Methods" menu 
2. Press the "Recharge" button 
3. Press the QRIS button then press the "TOPUP BALANCE" button 
4. Enter the phone number registered in digiRoad (example: 628123456xxxxxxx) 
5. Select the balance top-up nominal and click "Submit" 
6. Confirm the nominal and phone number that you entered then press "Continue"
7. A QR code will appear that you can scan through the mobile banking application for payment transactions 
8. Click "Already Paid", if successful you will receive a successful payment notification

For information that the process of adding a balance using QRIS you will be charged Rp 1,500 for administrative fees.
