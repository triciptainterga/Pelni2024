In connection with the question Mrs. Ryantirhy conveyed, we can inform you that to add a payment method in your Cantas account, you can follow these steps :

1. Select "Account" menu
2. Select "Payment Service"
3. Click the "Add Payment Method" button
4. Select the payment method of your choice
5. You can now use the payment method of your choice

And here are the steps to use the Cantas app:

1. Download the Cantas App on the Play Store or App Store
2. Register by entering your personal data and vehicle data.
3. Select and connect with the payment method in the application
4. Ensure sufficient balance with a minimum balance of Rp 20,000 in the Cantas application in order to travel
5. Ensure that the vehicle selected in the application matches the one in use
6. Make sure you have selected "Start Trip" before entering the toll road
7. The system will detect when the mother's vehicle crosses the toll road then the balance in the application will be automatically deducted according to the toll tariff that is passed.
