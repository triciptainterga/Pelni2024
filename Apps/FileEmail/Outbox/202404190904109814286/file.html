Regarding to your question about causes to getting fine in Cantas, We inform you of the conditions or violations that may occur and can cause you to be fined:
1. Entering the toll road without using the Cantas application
2. Not pressing the "Start Trip / Start Trip" button in the Cantas application before entering the toll road
3. Not using the appropriate / registered vehicle in the Cantas application
4. Choosing the wrong vehicle category in the Cantas application
5. Insufficient balance when using the toll road
6. Car is used by relatives or friends
