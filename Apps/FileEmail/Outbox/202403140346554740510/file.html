Madam, we can inform you that MLFF stands for "Multi Lane Free Flow" which is a contactless toll payment system that can be done without having to stop at the toll gate. This system is implemented in Indonesia through the CANTAS application with GNSS (Global Navigation Satellite System) technology that utilizes GPS on smart phones to track toll road users' trips.  For information, an internal trial of the MLFF system has been carried out in Bali at the Ngurah Rai Toll Gate (GT), Bali Mandara Toll Road on December 15, 2023, and in the future it will be gradually implemented in all toll roads in Indonesia.
