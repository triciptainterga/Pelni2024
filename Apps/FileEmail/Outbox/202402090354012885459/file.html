We can inform you that we will be using ney system called MLFF. MLFF stands for "Multi Lane Free Flow" which is a contactless toll payment system that can be done without having to stop at the toll gate. This system is implemented in Indonesia through the CANTAS app with GNSS technology which uses GPS on smartphones to track the journey of toll road users. Currently the CANTAS application is still in the trial phase on the Bali toll road, and in the future it will gradually be implemented on all toll roads in Indonesia. 

as for Cantas, Cantas is a smart application designed with (MLFF) or Multi Lane Free Flow system that utilize  (GNSS) or Global Navigation Satellite System technology  which allows vehicle users to make payments just by using their device without having to stop.  

if you are going to use the toll facility, you are required to register your personal data and your vehicle in the cantas application, both privately owned and rental vehicles. here are the steps to register your personal account : 

1. Click the "Sign up" button
2. Click the "Agree" button for Terms & Conditions
3. Fill in and complete your personal information (Name, Date of Birth, Address, User Name, Password)
4. Enter your phone number that is connected to your Whatsapp 
5. You will receive an OTP (One Time Password) code sent to your Whatsapp number.
6. Enter the OTP code to your Cantas application
7. Your Cantas account is ready to use

and these are the steps to register your vehicle 
1. Select the "Account" menu
2. Select the "Vehicle" menu
3. Click the "Add Vehicle" button or the Plus sign
4. Select the toll category that matches your vehicle
5. Enter your vehicle information, then click "Next"
6. Upload photos of your front and rear vehicle, then click "Next"
7. Upload photos of your front and back vehicle registration document
8. Click "Finish"
should you no longer be using the rental vehicle, you must delete the registered vehicle from your application to avoid things that are not desirable
