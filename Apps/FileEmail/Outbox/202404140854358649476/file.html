Very well, Ms. Kusuma Marisya. We are sorry to inform you that changing the username in the Cantas application is currently not possible. You can create a new Cantas account with another username that you want with another phone number that is connected to Whatsapp. 
If you want to create an account with a new username but still use the previous phone number, you can delete the account with a processing time of 15 days for permanent deletion. After deleting the account, information related to travel history, fine history, and other information previously recorded in your account will also be deleted.

And to change your mobile number, you can do the following:
1. Enter the ""Account"" menu
2. Select the ""Profile"" menu then the details of personal data will appear. Select ""Contact Details""
3. Select ""Change"" and enter the new cell phone number you want to use. 
4. Press the ""Next"" button and a pop-up message will appear asking whether the new cell phone number entered is correct or not. Press the ""OK"" button if it is correct.
5. The OTP code will be sent to the new mobile number you entered
