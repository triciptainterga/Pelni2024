We are sorry for the inconvenience, We would like to Inform Mrs Anggie how Cantas application works. Cantas is a smart application designed with (MLFF) or Multi Lane Free Flow. Here's how the MLFF system works:

-  Toll road users do registration in Cantas app
-  Toll road users have to press "Start" before doing the trip
-  User's position when entering and leaving the toll road will be captured from GNSS technology
-  When leaving the toll road, user's have to press "End" and the apps will calculate the cost based on initial and final position, then balance will be automatically deducted
-  The function of gantry is to identify any violence, number plate, vehicle category and number of vehicle passed
