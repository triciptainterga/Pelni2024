Regarding Mr. Fahmi Nur question to top up the balance through Digiroad using QRIS for bank account you can follow these steps: 
1. Select the "Payment Method" menu 
2. Press the "Top Up" button 
3. Press QRIS button then press "TOPUP BALANCE" button 3. 
4. Enter the Phone Number registered in digiRoad (example: 628123456xxxxxxx) 
5. Select the top up balance amount and click "Submit" 
6. Confirm the amount and phone number that you have entered and click "Continue". 
7. A QR code will appear that you can scan through the mobile banking application for payment transactions.
8. Click "Already Paid" if successful You will receive a notification that the payment has been successful For information that the process of adding a balance using QRIS You will be charged Rp 1,500 for administrative costs
